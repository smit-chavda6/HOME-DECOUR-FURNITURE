About Us

Your trusted source for quality furniture

WHO WE ARE

At Home Decor Furniture, we are passionate about creating beautiful spaces. With over a decade of experience, we offer a wide range of high-quality furniture designed to suit every style and budget. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the furniture industry.

We believe that every home tells a story, and we're here to help you write yours with furniture that combines beauty, functionality, and quality craftsmanship.

SUSTAINABILITY

We strive to use materials that are sustainably sourced, and strongly believe in supporting artisans and their crafts. As a brand, we aim to preserve and revive the dying traditions and techniques of handcrafted furniture making.

We are associated with NGOs supporting this goal, including 'The Vishwakarma Swayam Sahayata Samuh' that is engaged in helping and providing education & training to local artisans.
